Game Rules

  1. Match predictions will be available from 8 AM and will close 5 minutes before the toss.
  2. Users can modify their predictions within this time frame.
  3. Entry Fee per Match: Rs. 10
  4. Points Distribution:
    Prediction Factor Points
    Toss Winner 1
    Match Winner 5
    Man of the Match 5
    Most Runs 2
    Most Wickets 2
    Most Catches 2
    Most Sixes 3

    Note: In case of a tie, the prediction submitted first will be considered.

  5. Prize Distribution: Based on the number of participants:
    No. of Players First Prize Second Prize Third Prize
    {{ total.key }} {{ total.value.first }} {{ total.value.second }} {{ total.value.third }}

  6. Fantasy Cricket Points System

    1. Batting Points:

    • Runs Scored:
      • 1 point for every run scored.
    • Bonus for milestone:
      • 10 points for scoring 50 runs.
      • 25 points for scoring 100 runs.
    • For a Duck (0 runs):
      • Minus 2 points.
    • Boundaries and Sixes:
      • 2 points for every boundary (4).
      • 4 points for every six (6).

    2. Bowling Points:

    • Wickets Taken:
      • 25 points for every wicket taken.
    • Bonus for milestones:
      • 10 points for taking 3 wickets.
      • 25 points for taking 5 wickets.
    • For No Wickets:
      • Minus 2 points.
    • Economy Rate:
      • A bowler with an economy rate of 6 or less earns 4 points.
      • Economy rate between 6.1 to 8.5 earns 2 points.
      • Economy rate between 8.6 to 9 earns 0 points.
      • Economy rate above 9 loses 2 points.

    3. Fielding Points:

    • Catches:
      • 8 points for every catch taken.
    • Run-outs:
      • 12 points for a successful run-out.
    • Stumpings:
      • 12 points for a stumping.

    4. Bonus:

    • Captain: x2
    • Vice Captain: x1.5
    • Man of the match: 25 pts
